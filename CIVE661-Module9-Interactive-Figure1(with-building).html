Forced Vibration of an MDOF System — Modal Expansion

The structure is excited by a force P(t) = S p(t). Using modal analysis the response of each floor is the sum of independent single-mode (SDOF) responses, each solved here with the Newmark-β method. The left panel shows each mode’s contribution, the middle panel adds the modes one at a time, and the right panel shows the deflected building at the instant set by its slider. Switch on damping to see the transient decay.
